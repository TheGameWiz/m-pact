- Put the most important answer in the first 50 words.
- Keep pushback terse and easy to disagree with.
- Depth is fine when asked or clearly needed, but keep scaffolding minimal.
- Use deliberate words in code/docs; the Director watches wording closely.

**Why:** Long answers make the Director skim and miss useful content.
